Timing · Order · Soil · Sun · Companions — Oxford MI · Zone 5b–6a

🤝 Companion Planting Guilds

Three Sisters Corn · Beans · Squash / Pumpkin
Classic Iroquois guild — corn provides poles, beans fix nitrogen, squash shades weeds
Nightshade Bed Tomatoes · All Peppers · Basil
Peppers share nutrient/water needs; basil repels aphids & whitefly
Root & Cool Greens Carrots · Radishes · Lettuce · Onions
Radishes loosen soil for carrots; onions deter carrot fly
Pollinator Border Nasturtiums · Sunflowers · Milkweed
Attract pollinators, beneficial insects & monarchs to the whole garden
Cucurbit Patch Cucumber · Zucchini · Summer & Winter Squash · Butternut
Group near nasturtiums to deter squash bugs; space for sprawl
Potato Row Potatoes · Pontiac Red Potatoes · Beans · Horseradish
Keep away from tomatoes/peppers (same blight). Beans fix nitrogen
